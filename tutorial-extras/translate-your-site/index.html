SERHANT A.I Chat

This page operates through starting a chat by asking questions in the text box at the bottom of the page and then receiving a response from SGPT. The page has three prompts designed to start a chat and provoke you to ask questions.

Once an answer is generated a response will be shown as such. You are able to ask follow up questions as well as ‘Copy’ the generated response using the button .

Chat Threads​

All of the chats will be displayed on the left-side of the screen and will be saved. To clear the saved threads you can click on the ‘Clear Threads’ button .To start a new chat you can click on the ‘Create new Chat’ button .
